Overview

Highly Skilled and efficient Employees are the most important asset of any organization. Blueciate realizes this fundamental business truth and strives sincerely to provide you with the human resource support necessary to achieve your business Objectives.

--%>

Advantages of partnering with Blueciate

Solving the complex problems of talent acquisition, be it –consistency in career, capability & core competence, or analyzing the academics Blueciate would be instrumental to our clients in augmenting the workforce by attracting and retaining quality talent. We, at Blueciate , are committed to our clients by ensuring availability of Quality candidates meeting relevant skill sets, experience and educational qualifications. Blueciate provide value addition and quality in recruitment services with the shortest possible turnaround time with multiple skill sets across the country in a broad range of verticals including IT, Manufacturing and BPO.
Our recruiting team has years of industry experience and a proven record of providing highly qualified IT professionals. Blueciate has the competency to get your requirements fulfilled in cost effective and timely manner which is the key for our customers' expectations of getting the right candidate at the right time

Assurance about Quality

we conduct 2 rounds of tele interviews for all candidates for determining communication skills as well as technical abilities as per the guidelines of the client. These checks ensure that the unsuitable candidates are eliminated and our clients receive only candidates who meet their requirements. Speed : We have extensive contacts in the IT industry and would be in a position to address your growing manpower requirements appropriately by providing suitable candidates in the shortest possible time.

Checks on references

Blueciate believes in conducting reference checks on the candidates as per the guidelines of the Clients after the candidate reaches the offer letter stage. These checks assure our clients that they are recruiting genuine candidates with proper track record, both academically as well as career wise.

Approaching Ideal Candidates

After analyzing the parameters of the requirements with the skill sets and track record of candidates, we Evaluate candidates with the aim of determining a profile match with the job, their career aspirations and suitability in the context and culture of the client organization by explaining them about the opportunity and check out their interest in the position offered.

Follow-up after Offer

These days, many candidates have multiple offers. So , Blueciate believes in maintaining contacts with the candidate’s right through the notice period to doubly ensure that the candidates understand the career growth advantages of joining with the client. This doubly ensure that the offered candidates join the the clients and the Client's induction plans don't get derailed in the last hour.
